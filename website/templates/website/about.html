Who We Are

Tanaji Screen Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted leader in screen printing and laser engraving. Located in Additional MIDC Satara, Maharashtra, we have proudly served the industry for over 26 years since 1996.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility blends craftsmanship with modern technology. We serve clients across India and globally in China, Dubai, Thailand and Spain, delivering solutions that help our partners sharpen their competitive edge.

Founded by Mr. Tanaji Mohite, our organization has evolved to a systemic working approach with global-class Quality Assurance norms. We are committed to manufacturing and supplying the best quality products by continually improving the effectiveness of our quality management systems.
