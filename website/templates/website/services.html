Screen Printing Solutions

Screen printing is the process of pressing ink through a stenciled mesh screen to create precise, durable printed designs. This versatile technique is utilized across multiple industries for creating high-quality membrane keypads, flexible circuits, and graphic overlays.

Membrane Keypads & Flexible Circuits

Membrane keypads are the most widely used form of switch technology today, offering exceptional reliability, long life, and design flexibility. We manufacture conductive rubber switches and keypads that look and feel like traditional push-button switches without their inherent disadvantages.

Our flexible printed circuits serve as connectors in applications where flexibility, space savings, or production constraints limit the use of rigid circuit boards. These circuits can bend, fold, and move freely, making them ideal for mobile communication, notebooks, computer peripherals, PDAs, digital cameras, aerospace, and military applications.

Graphic Overlays

The graphic overlay serves as the visible representation of a device's user interface panel. It functions as the topmost layer, either as a single adhesive-backed layer atop a membrane switch or to activate micro switches. We design overlays that are both functionally effective and visually striking.

Our custom graphic overlays provide an excellent canvas for high-resolution graphics, enabling companies to showcase their branding and logos while maintaining durability. We use polyester for applications requiring millions of actuations and solvent resistance, while polycarbonate offers crisp design for less demanding applications.
